Kinderkinetics.

A profession that aims to promote and optimize the neuromotoric development of children aged 0-13 through scientifically based physical activity. It focuses on the unique developmental needs of every individual child and is a fun-orientated program that helps to develop a child's motor skills. All programmes within the profession have a preventative, stimulating, developmental and remedial nature.


Swimming.

We also provide swimming lessons as a means to achieving the above mentioned goals in Kinderkinetics.

What We Offer

What We Offer

Baby Stimulation Lessons

Stimulating exercises to enhance development, both gross motor and fine motor activities. Only until the child can walk.

Movement Lessons

Balance, rhythm and timing, locomotion, coordination, ball skills and spatial orientation. Starts when the child can walk until 13 years of age.

Wellness Lessons

Focuses on certain sport skills and is more fitness orientated. Examples include hockey drills, soccer, rugby, etc. Ages 6 to 13.

Swimming Lessons

Development of swimming skills for ages ranging between 7 months to 13 years. Basic to advanced aquatic skills depending on age and requirements. Parents/Guardians may climb in pool.

Remedial Lessons

Addresses physical challenges of gross motor movements to ensure readiness for school and improve school performance. Focuses on children’s individual and specific needs.
